About Me

C++ and Python software developer who loves solving problems and challenging the status quo. I love to automate solutions to improve work efficiency & decision making through effective future focused system design. My background and expertise consists of real-time streaming, sensor data analysis, image processing, and embedded systems development. I am also interested in Big Data and currently expanding my knowledge in Cloud-based data and distributed systems.
